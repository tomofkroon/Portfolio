About

I’m a creative, passionate, detail-obsessed front-end web developer looking to make the web a more user-friendly place one website at a time. I love the direction the web is going with new standards & technologies like HTML5, CSS3, jQuery and RESTful API’s. With all these new toys to play with, I’ve made it my obsession to figure out how I can make it easier for people to explore, create and share on the web.

When I’m not obsessing about web culture or working on a project, I’m usually playing board games, home brewing or having a beer with friends and talking about the latest comic book movies. I’ve lived in Seattle, WA my entire life, graduated from the University of Washington and worked for a time at the SeattlePI.com. I would have moved by now, but Seattle is such an awesome town that I haven’t come up with a good enough reason to go anywhere else!

I'm a front-end web developer at heart, but I've also worked as a SEO consultant, social media manager, content auditor and PPC campaign manager in the past.

I'm good at HTML, CSS, jQuery, PHP, MySQL, UX design and SEO

I'm learning about graphic design, JSON, mobile web design and SEM
